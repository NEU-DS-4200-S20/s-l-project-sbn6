Motivation

Our project is about assisting the Sustainable Business Network by creating a visual representation of their member database. SBN has demonstrated a need for an easy and visual way for them to connect their members with their prospective clients. Visualization explanation

Currently, we have our data being loaded onto a map of New England. Each point is the location of an SBN member business. The user has the ability to highlight these points for use in linking to future vizualisation(s). In addition, each state on the map is color-coded based on whether or not an SBN member business is present in that state: blue means there is, grey means there is not.
